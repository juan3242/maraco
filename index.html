KEEP THE COFFEE COMING

Enjoy the best and latest from Caravan Coffee Roasters in your own home. Get free delivery and a free Caravan Coffee Roasters gift when you sign-up for mighty fine coffee delivered on the regular. By joining, you’ll also be helping do some good and giving back to coffee growing communities. They've committed to support female coffee producers by sourcing over a third of their coffee from women and have joined 1% for the Planet, giving back 1% of their revenue each year to environmental charities. ⁠ JOIN THE WELL-TRAVELLED COFFEE JOURNEY

LOCATED ON A VIBRANT PEDESTRIANISED MARKET STREET, CARAVAN EXMOUTH MARKET IS A BRIGHT AND AIRY SPACE WITH GLAZED FRONTAGE ON TWO SIDES AND AN OUTSIDE TERRACE ON THE MARKET. SERVING OUR SIGNATURE WELL-TRAVELLED BREAKFAST AND ALL DAY FOOD AND DRINKS MENUS, ALONG WITH OUR FRESHLY ROASTED COFFEE FROM THE CARAVAN ROASTERY, OUR STYLE IS OPEN, RELAXED AND FRIENDLY. COME DOWN FOR A COFFEE WITH THE MORNING PAPER, A COCKTAIL AND SMALL PLATES IN THE EVENING AND ANYTHING IN BETWEEN!
